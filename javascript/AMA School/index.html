Welcome to AMA School

AMA School began as a nursery-cum-preparatory school in 2004 with just 60 pupils on its roll then. Today it is a co-educational school with classes Creche to XII (Science and Commerce, the two streams offered in +2) with English as the medium of instruction. Affiliated to C.B.S.E., New Delhi and having trained faculty and student strength of around 1100, the School is situated at Raja Park. It is managed by a registered Educational Society.
