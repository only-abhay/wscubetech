Nursery School

The Nursery School comprises Creche to Class Prep. This section housed in a separate wing has an exclusive large play ground attached. The method of instruction for this section is ‘Learning through Play, Explore and Discover’ and just 1 to 2 1/2 hours of the total 5 hour work day is devoted to actual class room studies. Regular visits are arranged to the Zoo, Parks, Magic Shows, Children Cinema and other such places of interest. It is real fun to visit the little children and watch them enjoy cycling, clay modeling, playing music and reciting rhymes. Very little home work or assignments are given.

AMA School of Management

AMA School of Management is located at Newai far from the madding crowd of the city but in the RIICO Industrial area Phase II, INS 2&3 thus keeping it well connected with the rest of the world. The campus spreads in 15 acres of land with the back drop of hills and hillocks of the Aravali Range and vast stretches of farmlands.
